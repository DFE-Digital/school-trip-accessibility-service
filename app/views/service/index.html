Give permission for your child to go on a school trip

This service enables you to:

  1. retrieve details of a school trip
  2. accept or reject the request for your child to go on the trip
  3. provide emergency contact details
  4. pay any due fees for the trip

The process takes about 5 minutes.

Information you'll need:

  • the trip reference number
  • your child's pupil number
  • you may need a credit or debit card if a fee is due
