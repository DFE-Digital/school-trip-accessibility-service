Read carefully before you continue

This service has been developed by the Department for Education as a training service.

This is not a real service and is not indicative of real services designed and built by government departments or organisations.

What the service is

This is a fictional service which enables parents and carers to give permission for their child to go on a school trip to a museum.

Why we have created this service

This service has deliberately been designed to be difficult to use, for example:

  1. the service has known accessibility issues
  2. there are usability issues
  3. there is language that is not considered inclusive

This service has been designed to help you learn and understand about accessibility, usability, and inclusive design issues in digital services.
